How it worksComment ça marcheHow it works

Gas is a fixed cost — it kills small trades. Slippage grows with size — it kills large ones. Between both lies a precise sweet spot.

OrnyTools solves √(2 × gas × TVL) live, using real blockchain data, to give you that number before every trade.

⚠️
Low liquidity pool — fees will be high regardless of trade size. Check if a more liquid alternative exists before trading.
