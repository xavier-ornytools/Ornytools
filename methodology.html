What the optimizer solvesCe que l'optimiseur résoutQué resuelve el optimizador

Every swap on an AMM carries two competing costs: a fixed gas cost that dominates small trades, and a slippage cost that grows with trade size. Plotted against trade size, their sum forms a U-curve with a minimum — the optimal trade size.Chaque swap sur un AMM implique deux coûts concurrents : un coût de gas fixe qui domine les petits trades, et un coût de slippage qui croît avec la taille du trade. Tracée en fonction de la taille du trade, leur somme forme une courbe en U avec un minimum — la taille de trade optimale.Every swap on an AMM carries two competing costs: a fixed gas cost that dominates small trades, and a slippage cost that grows with trade size. Plotted against trade size, their sum forms a U-curve with a minimum — the optimal trade size.

The optimizer finds the exact bottom of that U-curve for any pool, using the full constant-product math rather than a linearized approximation.L'optimiseur trouve le bas exact de cette courbe en U pour n'importe quelle pool, en utilisant les maths constant-product complètes plutôt qu'une approximation linéarisée.The optimizer finds the exact bottom of that U-curve for any pool, using the full constant-product math rather than a linearized approximation.

The key insight: the optimal size is not a function of gas alone. It depends on pool depth, fee tier, and — for V3 — the active liquidity at the current price. Two pools with the same TVL can have optimal sizes that differ by 5–10×.L'insight clé : la taille optimale n'est pas uniquement fonction du gas. Elle dépend de la profondeur de la pool, du niveau de frais, et — pour V3 — de la liquidité active au prix actuel. Deux pools avec le même TVL peuvent avoir des tailles optimales qui diffèrent de 5 à 10×.The key insight: the optimal size is not a function of gas alone. It depends on pool depth, fee tier, and — for V3 — the active liquidity at the current price. Two pools with the same TVL can have optimal sizes that differ by 5–10×.

The total cost function minimized is:La fonction de coût total minimisée est :La función de coste total minimizada es:

C(A) = G/A  +  A/(R+A)  +  fee
         ↑        ↑            ↑
      gas %   slippage %   pool fee %

where A is the trade size (USD), G is the gas cost (USD, fixed), R is the effective one-sided reserve (USD), and fee is the pool fee as a fraction.où A est la taille de trade (USD), G est le coût de gas (USD, fixe), R est la réserve unilatérale effective (USD), et fee est le frais de pool en fraction.where A is the trade size (USD), G is the gas cost (USD, fixed), R is the effective one-sided reserve (USD), and fee is the pool fee as a fraction.

Why √(G·TVL) underestimatesPourquoi √(G·TVL) sous-estimeWhy √(G·TVL) underestimates

A commonly cited shortcut for the optimal trade size is the closed-form solution derived by linearizing slippage. Assuming A ≪ R (trade size much smaller than pool depth):Un raccourci souvent cité pour la taille de trade optimale est la solution analytique dérivée en linéarisant le slippage. En supposant A ≪ R (taille de trade bien inférieure à la profondeur de la pool) :A commonly cited shortcut for the optimal trade size is the closed-form solution derived by linearizing slippage. Assuming A ≪ R (trade size much smaller than pool depth):

Approximate slippage:  A/(R+A)  ≈  A/R   (when A ≪ R)

C(A) ≈ G/A + A/R + fee

dC/dA = −G/A²  +  1/R  = 0

A_opt ≈ √(G·R)  =  √(G · TVL/2)

For example, with G = $2.50 and TVL = $5M:Par exemple, avec G = $2,50 et TVL = $5M :For example, with G = $2.50 and TVL = $5M:

A_opt = √(2.50 × 2,500,000) = √6,250,000 ≈ $2,500

03 — Exact solver03 — Solveur exact03 — Solucionador exacto

Constant-product ternary searchRecherche ternaire constant-productConstant-product ternary search

Rather than approximating, OrnyTools minimizes the exact cost function numerically via ternary search — a derivative-free method for unimodal functions that converges in O(log n) iterations.Plutôt qu'approximer, OrnyTools minimise la fonction de coût exacte numériquement via une recherche ternaire — une méthode sans dérivée pour les fonctions unimodales qui converge en O(log n) itérations.Rather than approximating, OrnyTools minimizes the exact cost function numerically via ternary search — a derivative-free method for unimodal functions that converges in O(log n) iterations.

The exact cost modelLe modèle de coût exactThe exact cost model

// Exact slippage from constant-product AMM (x·y = k)
// One-sided reserve R = effective depth (USD)
// Trade of size A pushes the pool by A tokens

slippage_USD(A, R)  = A² / (R + A)
gas_share(A)        = G / A
fee_cost(A)         = fee × A

C(A) = G/A  +  A/(R+A)  +  fee   ← cost per dollar traded

Ternary searchRecherche ternaireTernary search

The cost function C(A) is strictly unimodal on (0, ∞): it decreases from infinity as gas dominates at small A, reaches a minimum, then increases as slippage dominates at large A. This guarantees ternary search converges to the global minimum.La fonction de coût C(A) est strictement unimodale sur (0, ∞) : elle décroît depuis l'infini lorsque le gas domine pour les petits A, atteint un minimum, puis croît lorsque le slippage domine pour les grands A. Cela garantit que la recherche ternaire converge vers le minimum global.The cost function C(A) is strictly unimodal on (0, ∞): it decreases from infinity as gas dominates at small A, reaches a minimum, then increases as slippage dominates at large A. This guarantees ternary search converges to the global minimum.

// Ternary search on [lo, hi] — O(log n) evaluations
lo = 0.01, hi = R × 0.5

while hi − lo > 0.01:
    m1 = lo + (hi − lo) / 3
    m2 = hi − (hi − lo) / 3
    if C(m1) < C(m2):
        hi = m2
    else:
        lo = m1

A_opt = (lo + hi) / 2

The search converges to $0.01 precision in under 50 iterations. There is no closed-form derivative required, and the result is exact for any R value — including the small active-liquidity values typical of tight V3 ranges.La recherche converge à une précision de $0,01 en moins de 50 itérations. Aucune dérivée analytique n'est requise, et le résultat est exact pour toute valeur de R — y compris les faibles valeurs de liquidité active typiques des ranges V3 serrés.The search converges to $0.01 precision in under 50 iterations. There is no closed-form derivative required, and the result is exact for any R value — including the small active-liquidity values typical of tight V3 ranges.

Fee tier inclusionInclusion des frais de tierFee tier inclusion

The pool fee fee × A is a linear term in total cost. Its derivative is constant, so it does not shift the minimum of the cost-per-dollar curve — it only raises the floor uniformly. The fee is included in the displayed total cost but does not change A_opt. This is a known property of proportional fee models and applies equally to 0.05%, 0.3% and 1% fee tiers.Le frais de pool fee × A est un terme linéaire dans le coût total. Sa dérivée est constante, donc il ne déplace pas le minimum de la courbe de coût-par-dollar — il ne fait que relever le plancher uniformément. Le frais est inclus dans le coût total affiché mais ne modifie pas A_opt. C'est une propriété connue des modèles de frais proportionnels et s'applique également aux niveaux de frais de 0,05%, 0,3% et 1%.The pool fee fee × A is a linear term in total cost. Its derivative is constant, so it does not shift the minimum of the cost-per-dollar curve — it only raises the floor uniformly. The fee is included in the displayed total cost but does not change A_opt. This is a known property of proportional fee models and applies equally to 0.05%, 0.3% and 1% fee tiers.

RPC fallback chainChaîne de fallback RPCRPC fallback chain

All on-chain reads go through a fallback RPC array per chain. If the primary endpoint fails or returns 0x, the engine retries on the next endpoint.
